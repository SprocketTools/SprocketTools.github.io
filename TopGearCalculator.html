Welcome!

The SprocketTools Gear Calculator is designed to make Sprocket engines easier than reading this text. Simply fill out statistics about your tank and click the button to get tailoorange results!

  • Cylinder size should match the in-game slider for cylinder volume.
  • Tuning style affects whether your vehicle tries to get up to speed quickly, or slowly accelerates to get as much speed as possible.
  • Climb angle determines the intended maximum slope that can be climbed, in degrees.
  • Max HP should be set to the highest RPM possible with your engine's upshift RPM.
  • If your vehicle struggles to turn, make sure that gears R1 and G1 match. Increasing their size, and reducing your sprocket diameter, also helps with turning.
